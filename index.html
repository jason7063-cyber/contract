114學年度外籍英語教師聘僱合約更新重點

Key Updates to the 114 School Year TFETP Employment Contract

本章節說明「聘僱起始起薪日」定義的調整，並新增條款以釐清薪資與福利的計算始點，確保權責分明。
This section explains the adjustments to the definition of the "start date of employment and salary" and adds new clauses to clarify the starting point for salary and benefit calculations, ensuring clear rights and responsibilities.

聘僱起始起薪日 (Start Date of Employment and Salary)

113學年度 (Previous)

定義較為繁瑣，包含取得工作許可、完成檢疫措施、在教育部或甲方指定地點完成報到手續當日起算。
The definition was more cumbersome, including obtaining a work permit, completing quarantine measures, and completing check-in procedures at a location designated by the MOE or Party A.

114學年度新合約 (New) ✨

定義簡化為：
The definition is simplified to:

乙方取得教育部核發之工作許可或內政部移民署核發之外僑永久居留證抑或依親居留證，並依甲方規定完成報到手續當日起算。
The date on which Party B obtains a work permit issued by the MOE, or a permanent or dependent resident certificate issued by the National Immigration Agency, and completes the check-in procedures as required by Party A.

第二條 (Art. 2)

【新增】薪資福利計算始點 (New Clause: Starting Point for Salary Calculation)

114學年度新合約 (New)

明確增訂：「聘僱起始起薪日為乙方實際提供勞務之始期；乙方之薪資及相關福利，亦自聘僱起始起薪日起計算，於聘僱起始起薪日前，甲方無義務支付任何薪資、福利或其他報酬」。
A new clause explicitly states: "The start date of employment and salary shall be the beginning of the actual provision of labor by Party B; Party B's salary and related benefits shall also be calculated from the start date of employment and salary. Before this date, Party A has no obligation to pay any salary, benefits, or other remuneration."

第二條 (Art. 2)

教師的責任與義務在114學年度合約中有更廣泛且明確的定義，包含工作職責、寒暑假活動內容以及健康檢查的要求皆有調整。
The responsibilities and obligations of teachers are more broadly and clearly defined in the 114 SY contract, including adjustments to job duties, activities during winter and summer breaks, and health check requirements.

工作職責範圍 (Scope of Work)

113學年度 (Previous)

執行「教學相關活動等工作」。
Perform "teaching-related activities, etc."

工作職責範圍 - 更新 (Update)

114學年度 (New)

擴大為「教學相關活動、研習課程、行政、輔導訪視相關作業等工作」。
Expanded to "teaching-related activities, training courses, administrative tasks, and counseling visit-related work, etc."

第三條 3.2

寒暑假工作內容 (Work during Breaks)

113學年度 (Previous)

教學、教學計畫撰寫、學生英語社團活動、教師研習活動等。
Teaching, writing lesson plans, student English club activities, teacher training activities, etc.

寒暑假工作內容 - 更新 (Update)

114學年度 (New)

新增甲方可要求乙方「運用非正式課程時間與學生進行英語對話、英語營隊等英語教學相關課程與活動教學事項」。
Party A may now require Party B to "use non-formal curriculum time to conduct English conversations with students, English camps, and other English teaching-related courses and activities."

經甲方同意於課餘時間施行之英語教學相關課程與活動，得計入每週基本授課節數之計算。
Such activities, with Party A's consent, can be counted towards the weekly basic teaching hours.

第三條 3.3

健康檢查要求 (Health Check) - 更新 (Update)

113學年度 (Previous)

乙方於契約簽訂後「即至所在地合格設立之醫療機構辦理健康檢查」。
Party B shall undergo a health check at a qualified local medical institution "after signing the contract".

114學年度 (New)

  • 健康檢查結果須「於報到前提供甲方」。
    The health check results must be "provided to Party A before check-in".
  • 新增：在職期間之健康檢查當日，得依相關證明文件，每學年至多請 公假1日。
    New: During the employment period, Party B is entitled to a maximum of 1 day of official leave per school year for health checks, with supporting documents.
第三條 3.8

薪資與福利是本次合約調整的重點。此處詳列了未滿月薪資計算、年資採計新規定、考核機制、各項補助的調整，以及新增的費用補助項目。
Salary and benefits are the focus of this contract adjustment. This section details new regulations for calculating pro-rata salary, seniority recognition, evaluation mechanisms, adjustments to various subsidies, and newly added reimbursable expenses.

【新增】未滿月薪資計算 & 年資採計 (New: Pro-rata Salary & Seniority Recognition)

  • 未滿月薪資：新增條款，服務未滿整月者，按當月實際在職日數覈實計支；其每日計發金額，以當月全月薪給除以三十日計算。
    Pro-rata Salary: A new clause states that if service is less than a full month, salary will be calculated based on the actual number of days worked.
  • 年資採計及改敘：新增獨立小節，詳細說明初任、在職期間取得較高學歷、轉換學校時的敘薪原則，以避免年資重複採計。
    Seniority Recognition & Res-sessment: A new subsection details salary principles for initial appointments, for obtaining higher academic qualifications during employment, and for transfers between schools to prevent duplicate seniority calculations.
第四條 4.1.1 & 4.1.2

考核獎懲 (Evaluation) - 更新 (Update)

  • 明確考核項目：調整為應包含「教學專業、行政配合、專業倫理」。
    Clarified Evaluation Criteria: Adjusted to include "teaching professionalism, administrative cooperation, and professional ethics."
  • 新增輔導機制：倘任一項應考核項目未滿70分，或考核評等為乙等以下者，應配合所屬地方政府及甲方協助之相關輔導並改善。
    New Support Mechanism: If any item scores below 70 or the rating is Grade B or lower, Party B must cooperate with counseling and improvement measures assisted by the local government and Party A.
第四條 4.2

交通補助 (Transportation Subsidy) - 更新

  • 直系親屬定義依本國民法相關規定辦理。
    Definition of lineal relatives is based on R.O.C. Civil Code.
  • 回程機票核銷期限明確修改為「聘僱起始起薪日起至該學年結束1個月內（8月31日前）」。
    Reimbursement deadline for return tickets is shortened to "within 1 month after the end of the school year (before Aug 31st)."
第四條 4.3

租屋補助 (Housing Subsidy) - 更新

新增條款「倘乙方受聘僱期間之首月或末月租屋期間未足月者，仍以月計，並依前項規定核實補助」。
New clause ensures that if the rental period in the first or last month of employment is not a full month, the subsidy will still be calculated on a monthly basis.

第四條 4.4

保險 (Insurance) - 更新

明確全民健康保險適用對象為「須領有居留證明文件者」，並訂定意外事故保險的生效日期為「聘僱起始起薪日起」。
Clarifies that NHI applies to those "with a residence certificate," and sets the effective date of accident insurance to the "start date of employment."

第四條 4.5

其他費用 (Other Fees) - 更新

新增補助「文件驗證」等必要文件所生費用。
Adds reimbursement for expenses incurred from necessary documents such as "document authentication."

第四條 4.7

為更符合法規，114學年度合約調整了部分休假用語，使其與勞動基準法及相關辦法之規範一致。
To better align with regulations, the 114 SY contract adjusts some leave terminology to be consistent with the Labor Standards Act and related rules.

特別休假 (Special Leave) - 用語調整 (Terminology Adjustment)

113學年度 (Previous)

每年享有10天「給薪年假」。
10 days of "paid annual leave" per year.

114學年度 (New)

調整為每年享有10天「特別休假」。
Adjusted to 10 days of "special leave" per year.

第五條 5.1

本節說明合約終止與違約條款的更新，包含擴大違約認定範圍，以及在特定情況下對教師返國費用的保障。
This section covers updates to contract termination and breach clauses, including an expanded definition of breach and protections for the teacher's return travel costs in specific situations.

提供不實資料 (Providing False Info) - 更新

違約認定範圍新增「或失效資料者，且情節重大者」。
The scope of breach is expanded to include "providing invalid information, where the circumstances are significant."

第十二條 12.1.5

違反性別平等法令 - 更新

違反「性騷擾防治法」的條文編號已自第20條或第25條規定更新為第25條或第27條。
The article numbers in the "Sexual Harassment Prevention Act" have been updated from Art. 20/25 to Art. 25/27.

第十二條 12.1.8

【新增】甲方歸責提前終止契約之保障 (New Protection for Early Termination by Party A)

新增規定，若因可歸責於甲方之嚴重事由（如嚴重侮辱）導致乙方終止契約，甲方應依第4.3條規定負擔乙方返國機票費用，「如服務未滿6個月亦適用」。
A new provision states that if Party B terminates the contract due to serious reasons attributable to Party A (e.g., gross insult), Party A must cover the return airfare as per Art. 4.3, "even if the service period is less than 6 months."

第十三條 13.3

其他條款與附錄部分，主要針對續約評估、教學合作及行政配合等方面，提出了更具體的要求與說明。
This section covers other clauses and appendices, mainly providing more specific requirements for contract renewal assessment, teaching collaboration, and administrative cooperation.

續約評估 (Contract Renewal) - 更新

甲方應確認乙方符合資格，並「參酌」本契約第4.2所規定之考核結果並經地方政府同意後，依新契約條款續辦。
The renewal condition is changed from "conforming to" the evaluation results to "with reference to" the evaluation results, giving Party A more flexibility.

第十九條 19.2

教學準備 (Teaching Preparation) - 更新

新增要求乙方須與甲方協同教師於每堂課及每學期開始前充分討論及「共同備課」，確定課程與教材並擬定課程計畫，始得實施。
A new requirement for teachers to engage in "co-planning" with co-teachers before each class and semester.

附錄A 2.2

【新增】附錄A：運用非正式課程時間 (New Clause: Use of Non-formal Curriculum Time)

新增條目「乙方須與甲方共同規劃及運用非正式課程時間，辦理與學生進行英語對話之活動，例如英語口說學習角、英語營隊等英語教學相關活動，以提升學生英文聽說能力」。
A new item requires Party B to co-plan and use non-formal curriculum time for activities like "English Corners" or "English Camps" to improve students' speaking and listening skills.

附錄A 2.3

附錄B：薪資待遇標準表 - 更新 (Update)

114學年度的薪資待遇標準表，整體薪資級距有顯著調升。例如，學士學位、未滿1年年資的月薪從113學年度的NT$67,840提高到114學年度的NT$69,880。
The salary scale for the 114 SY shows a significant overall increase. For example, the monthly salary for a teacher with a bachelor's degree and less than one year of experience has been raised from NT$67,840 to NT$69,880.
